Hover a rate to isolate it; click to pin, click again to release. Fast = single deploy 20 fps, or dual deploy drogue 100 fps + main 20 fps. Slow = single deploy 10 fps, or dual deploy drogue 80 fps + main 10 fps.

Descent-only drift per model (GFS/HRRR/ECMWF/ICON/ARPEGE/GEM — only models with real pressure-level wind), integrated from apogee to ground, wind sampled every 50 ft of descent. Zone = convex hull of all models' points at that altitude, for both rate variants shown (single: 10/20 fps, dual: drogue 80/100 fps, main 10/20 fps). Point color = model, point shape = rate (circle = faster, square = slower) — hover any point for exact numbers. Not a go/no-go call — for a launch director to read against the actual approved landing zone once available.
